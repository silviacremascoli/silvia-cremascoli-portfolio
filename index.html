About me

What about me? Well, I love languages (spoken by humans or not 🤖), technology, and improving things for the better. That's why after working many years as a translator and content manager, I decided to embark on a new journey to learn programming languages and I became a committed front-end developer 💻 Since a translator must be particularly precise in the delivery of words and meanings into another language, I bring the same accuracy and attention to detail into my code. So, you can rest assured that the final result of your web project will be 99% (give me some slack!) the way you want it 😉 I am also committed to constant learning and personal development, because monotony is just too boring 😁
